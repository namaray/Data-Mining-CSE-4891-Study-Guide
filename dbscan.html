DBSCAN Algorithm

Density-Based Spatial Clustering of Applications with Noise.

Key Concepts

  • Epsilon (ε): The radius around a point. (The "reach").
  • MinPts: The minimum number of points required inside that radius to form a dense region.
  • Core Point: Has ≥ MinPts within its radius.
  • Border Point: Has < MinPts, but is a neighbor of a Core Point.
  • Noise Point: Has < MinPts and is NOT a neighbor of a Core Point.
